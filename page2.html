Sengkang ActiveSG Gym

Sengkang ActiveSG Gym, formerly known as Sengkang ClubFITT Gym, is a public gym operated by Sport Singapore. Had a long stressful day at work or been missing your working-out sessions? It’s crunch time! Let’s head down to the Sengkang ActiveSG Gym available in your neighbourhood today!
